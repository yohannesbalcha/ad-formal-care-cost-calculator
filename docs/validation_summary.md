# Validation Summary

The final model was evaluated using apparent performance, bootstrap optimism correction, repeated patient-level split validation, and internal temporal validation.

Key validation findings from the development study:

- Overall observed mean annual formal-care cost was close to the model-predicted mean.
- Observed and predicted state-specific mean costs differed by less than 3%.
- Bootstrap optimism was small for mean absolute error and root mean squared error.
- Repeated patient-level split validation showed stable mean calibration.
- Internal temporal validation showed modest underprediction in later calendar years, supporting periodic recalibration when applying the cost function to future cohorts.
